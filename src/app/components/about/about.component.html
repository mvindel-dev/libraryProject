About Marc's library

Welcome to Marc's library, a place dedicated to fostering a love for literature and knowledge. Our library has been a cornerstone of the community since 2009.

Our Mission

At Marc's library, our mission is to provide a welcoming space for readers of all ages and backgrounds. We aim to promote literacy, lifelong learning, and a sense of community through our diverse collection of books and engaging events.

Services

Explore our extensive collection of books, including fiction, non-fiction, children's literature, and more. We offer a variety of services, including:

  • Book borrowing
  • Author readings and book signings
  • Children's storytelling sessions
  • Book club discussions
  • Community events and workshops

Visit Us

We're located at Lleida. Visit us during our regular hours to discover the joy of reading and learning. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you in finding the perfect book.
